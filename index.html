While there I realized I did not see a future in Biology and wanted to find a new path.

One day my Grandfather was visiting and he mentioned a school he heard about on the radio called code fellows. Computers and the tech industry were something that had always interested me and my Grandpa believed this was my chance to take my life in a whole new direction so here I am.
